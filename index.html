What does Water Guardians do?

It implements web applications for integrated management of local water resources (Morelia) by gathering information to know the temporal variations of water levels and to predict water resources availability, as well as to plan strategies for their management.

What does the system provides

The Water Guardians platform provides integrated visual information on local water resources.

  • Water Mains
  • Surface water
  • Weather Forecasts

With this information we will probide weather forecast and suggest planned startegies do farmers and goverment sector in order to preserve hidric.
